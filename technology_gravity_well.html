The Technology Gravity Well

The concept of the "Technology Gravity Well" explores how technological advancements can either centralize or decentralize power and wealth, depending on how those technologies expand markets. When technology operates in a zero-sum environment, it centralizes resources and increases inequality, acting as a "centralized technology gravity well." Conversely, technologies that create non-zero sum environments decentralize power, creating a "decentralized technology gravity well."

This hypothesis is critical in understanding the future economic trajectories of nations, particularly as automation reduces the need for human labor in many industries.
