The Change the World University Challenge (Phase 0)

The “Change the World University Challenge” will initially offer a prize for the best short essay on the concept of a general collective intelligence platform or the concepts of the “collective social brain”, or the “technology gravity well” that are essential to understanding it.

This phase sets the foundation for subsequent phases, encouraging participants to deeply explore the key theories of the contest.
