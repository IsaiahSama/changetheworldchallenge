The Change the World University Challenge (Phases 1-10)

In Phases 1-10, the challenge expands to include real-world application of the principles learned in Phase 0.

Participants will collaborate in teams to design a network of interventions that have the greatest impact on Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) targeted by the challenge.
