Strong or General Collective Intelligence

Strong or General Collective Intelligence (strong CI) refers to the hypothetical ability of human groups to optimally define problems and discover algorithmic solutions beyond the limitations of traditional decision-making models. Strong CI mirrors the concept of strong AI in that it embodies the capacity to solve a broad range of problems, beyond narrow, domain-specific challenges.

In this framework, network effects allow solutions to cooperate, amplifying the overall impact. This model is predicted to vastly improve problem-solving for complex societal challenges by combining the strengths of both intuitive and logical reasoning across large groups.
